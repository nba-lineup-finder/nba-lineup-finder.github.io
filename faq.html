Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is this app?

This app helps you manage and analyze player lineups. You can select teams and players to see their performance stats.

How do I select players?

To select players, choose a team from the dropdown and then pick players to include or exclude from your lineup.

Can I download the results?

Yes, once you generate lineups, you can download the results as a CSV file.

Who do I contact if I have issues?

If you have any questions or issues, you can contact us via the Contact page.
